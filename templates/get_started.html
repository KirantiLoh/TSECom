Getting Started

Here's what should you know when you are using this website

  1. This is just a mockup
  2. You start with Rp 100.000.000,00 everytime you make an account
  3. You lose money when you buy stuff (duh)
  4. You earn money by selling items
  5. You can see all of the products you sell via checking your order
  6. You can see all of the products you sell via checking your own profile
  7. You can leave a review to a product after you bought it (But not your own items bruh)
  8. Lastly, have fun!

Login And Register

Being an user in this site enables you to buy, sell, and leave reviews. Fear not, we will teach you how to do it in this website

Desktop

  1. Click Login (On the top right corner)
  2. Fill in the form
  3. Congrats you have just added a new product!

Mobile

  1. Click the menu (Located at the top right corner)
  2. Click Login
  3. Fill in the form
  4. Congrats you have just added a new product!

Buying Products

Maybe you have found a product that you want to buy. Fear not, we will teach you how to do it in this website

Desktop

  1. Click the product's name, and you'll be redirected to the product page
  2. Enter the amount you want to buy, and you'll be redirected to your Cart
  3. Click the checkout button
  4. Congrats you have just bought a new product!

Mobile

  1. Click the product's name, and you'll be redirected to the product page
  2. Click the up arrow button, and a
  3. Enter the amount you want to buy, and you'll be redirected to your Cart
  4. Click the checkout button
  5. Congrats you have just bought a new product!
